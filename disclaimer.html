Information about the academic purpose, copyright status, and limitations of this web exhibition.

01

Academic Purpose

The purpose of this web site is to explore various types of typographic and layout style for specialized tourist visits, as an end-of-course project for the "Information Modeling and Web technologies" course of the Master's Degree in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge of the University of Bologna, under the supervision of prof. Fabio Vitali.

This exhibition was created entirely for academic and educational purposes. It does not represent an official publication of the University of Bologna, the films' production companies, or any of the locations featured. All film titles, character names, and production details are the property of their respective rights holders.

03

Accuracy & Limitations

Location information and GPS coordinates are provided in good faith for educational purposes. Visitors intending to follow these routes should verify access and opening hours directly with each site, as these may change.

Narrative texts represent interpretive readings of the films and their locations, not authoritative production records. Scene descriptions are based on publicly available information about filming locations.

Historical and architectural information included in narrative notes is presented in summary form for contextual purposes. Readers seeking academic-level detail should consult primary historical sources.

Some locations featured in films may have restricted public access. This exhibition does not encourage trespass or unauthorized entry to any private or restricted premises.
